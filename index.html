Hi! Welcome to Canada's Marvels!

We are Christof and Eva and we arrived in Ontario, Canada a bit less over a year ago. We have always loved to travel, but have never lived abroad. Living in Canada is a dream come true and with all the adventures at our fingertips, our moto is to discover as many marvels as possible in the time we live in this beautiful country. So whether you want to get inspired, motivated or just want to see what we are up to, this is a place to look. We are also always very eager to learn about new and exciting places in Ontario or Canada.
